Home Sweet Home

A Cozy Find the Difference Adventure

Game Features

10 Beautiful Rooms

Explore charming home interiors and test your observation skills with our carefully crafted scenes.

Progressive Difficulty

Start with simple differences and advance to more challenging levels as you improve.

Hint System

Stuck? Use our helpful hint system to guide you through tricky spots.

How to Play

  1. Compare the two images carefully
  2. Tap on the differences you spot
  3. Use hints if you get stuck (tap the magnifying glass)
  4. Complete all levels to unlock new rooms
  5. Try to beat your best time!
